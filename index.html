Blazers: 55% Polyester, 45% Wool

Machine Wash on wool cycle or, hand wash using detergent recommended for wool only.

Dry cleanable with care P.

Wash dark colours separately.

Do not bleach.

Do not tumble dry.

Caution: Do not mix loads with towelling or brushed fabrics. Do not mix dark colours or light colours. Do not use detergents containing perborate.
